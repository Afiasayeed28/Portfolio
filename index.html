My Work Process

As an L&D manager, I orchestrate a multidisciplinary team of 14 across the full lifecycle of emotion-centric learning experiences for global clients.

We start from scratch — connecting with clients, understanding their training needs, creating TNAs from conversations, brainstorming with SMEs, and building comprehensive project plans. My IDs own their courses while graphic designers craft mood boards and visual systems, and QAs ensure every deliverable meets the bar.

01
Change Management · SAP · Accenture

SAP S/4HANA Migration — WalkMe Smart Walkthroughs

WalkMeSAP Enable NowSAP FiorivILTWBTHLD
Multi-StreamPTP, OTC, RTR
GlobalSME Collaboration
Real-TimeOn-Screen Guidance
The Business Problem
A large enterprise was migrating to SAP S/4HANA — thousands of employees needed to adopt new workflows across Procure-to-Pay, Order-to-Cash, and Record-to-Report streams. Traditional training wasn't keeping up, leading to user frustration and support ticket overload.
My Approach
Led change management workstreams by implementing WalkMe for real-time Smart Walkthroughs and on-screen guidance inside SAP Fiori. Partnered with functional leads across streams to build just-in-time learning. Created comprehensive HLD documents, content outlines, and validation decks for WBTs, ILTs, and vILTs.
The Outcome
Seamless user adoption during complex software transitions — support tickets dropped significantly and employees could self-serve through guided walkthroughs that scaled across global rollouts.
